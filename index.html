Welcome to Billy Hawk Caye

We welcome you to Billy Hawk Caye Lodge. Operated mainly as a fishing island through its early years, the Sabals have worked to build the island up over the past fifteen years to invite tourism. Billy Hawk Caye as we know it is the only Garifuna cultural island in Belize and possibly in the world which is yet still true to their roots. This island paradise is conveniently located fourteen miles southeast of the culture capital Dangriga town, and nine miles east of Hopkins village. Alex and his sister, Jackie along with their extended family spends a good portion of their years on the island, still fishing when not entertaining guests.

Come For The Experience

Billy Hawk Caye is located on Belize's barrier reef, the second largest of its kind in the world. With its prime location, Billy Hawk Caye offers a number of activities that will allow you to enjoy and appreciate the nature around you. During your visit, you may wish to snorkel our surrounding reef, either by walking in off of our beach, or by boating to a nearby patch reef. Included in your visit is also the opportunity to spend time learning to fish the local reefs. Once you return to the island, if you'd like you can work with Jackie to learn to prepare what you've caught.
